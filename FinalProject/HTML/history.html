History

District Comedy is a comedy troupe located in Alexandria, Virginia. It was founded in 2020 by Thomas Beheler. Thomas set out to start this theater company after having worked on a professional troupe (Free Range Improv) for seven years, and as a professional actor for six. He is also a company member with Synetic Theater, where he also works as a Teaching Artist. District Comedy has performed in Maryland, Virginia, and D.C. They currently hold three awards for Best Local Show, Best Comedy Show, and a Helen Hayes Award for Outstanding Ensemble.
